开头段

第一句

  • With regard to…(n./how to…/whether…/the saying),viewpoints vary considerably 关于….，有很多观点
  • In the persent age,whether…has sparked much debate. 在当代，是否..引起很多讨论
  • There has been much discussion revolving around the issue of (whether…) 有很多讨论关于….问题

第二句

  • From my perspective/standpoint,
    • I’m in line with the arguement that
    • I reckon that … due to following reasons
    • it is advisable/wise to do that

中间段

主旨句

There are myriad number of reasons(why…)/method(how…)/题干东西(what…)

道理论证

  • First and foremost/Initially,the prime +题干东西 is undeniably …
  • secondly,and perhaps more importantly,the other noteworthy +题干东西 is …

后续衔接

  • To explain it further,This is due to the fact that
  • To be more specific ,/To clarify 更具体的是….

小结句

  • Accordingly,
    • it reinforce my point that
    • it is utterly mainfest that…

结尾段

第一步

To sum up/In a nut shell,

  • in light of the aforesaid factors… 鉴于前面提到过的这些因素
  • from what has been discussed above….

第二步

  • I reiterate my stand point that… reiterate 重申
  • it is natural to reach the point that
